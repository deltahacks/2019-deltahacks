HACKING

Teams of up to four are allowed, teams of one are discouraged. Hacking begins at 12PM on Saturday, January 16 and ends at 12PM on Sunday, January 17. All hacks must be submitted to ChallegePost to be considered for prizes. At that time, you can also check any Sponsor Challenge prizes you wish to be considered for.

Judging

After hacking ends, we will all move to Student Centre 1st Floor [Colored area on the map] for the expo judging. Each team will be provided a table and a table number. Please update your DevPost with your table number to help sponsor challenge judges find your hacks. Power will only be provided for hardware hacks, so please charge your devices before moving to expo area.

Judging consists of 3 rounds:

  1. Science fair expo: Each hack will be assigned a number of general judges.
    You only present at one of the expos.
    After your hack is submited on devpost you will be assigned a table number and expo number that will be posted on deltahacks.com/live
  2. Top 6 Presentations: The top 6 hacks will be selected from the first round of judging. These hacks will present to our panel of panel judges at Closing Ceremony.
  3. Grand Prizes (1st, 2nd, 3rd): Announced at the end of Closing Ceremony.
    Sponsor Challenge Prizes will be judged separately and announced (when possible) at the Closing Ceremony as well.

HARDWARE

Hardware is available for use. They may be checked out at Student Centre Rm 301 and must be returned immediately after judging. List of available hardware: https://mlh.io/hardware-lab

FAQ

    I Want to Sleep...

    Unfortunately, our venue does not meet firecode standards to have large amounts of students sleeping overnight. Thus, we ask that you don't sleep during the event. If you want to put your head down and take a quick nap, that's ok. But if we see you lying on the ground, we will have to wake you up.

    How do I connect to Wifi?

    Use MacSecure if you're a McMaster student. Use EduRoam if you are a Outside University student. If you are not a university student, please come pick up a wifi pass at Student Centre 1st floor [info desk]

    I don't have a team

    On Saturday at 12pm there will be an on going team forming session at Student 1st floor till 1:30pm to help pair anyone who doesn't have a team. Otherwise, try posting in the facebook particpants group: fb.com/groups/1671195733137406/

    Whom can I ask technical questions?

    Mentors are here to help you with any tech questions you might have. If you'd like to submit a request for a mentor to help you with something, please visit the mentor app.
